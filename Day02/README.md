# Dilution Calculator #
## The calculator: ##
* This calculator is a simplified version of other tools that help users determine the right concentrations or volumes needed for preparing working buffers, acids, and chemical solutions from concentrated stock.
* This calculator is based on the equation: **C1xV1 = C1xV2**.  
* This calculation is really useful when need to know how much stock solution (V1) to take to prepare a specific volume (V2) of a weaker solution (C2). It can also be helpful when the stock solution (C1) is known and you want to determine how much to dilute it to reach a desired final concentration.
  
## Example: ##
You want to prepare **100 mL** of a **0.5 M NaCl** solution from a **2 M stock**

C1 = 2 M, **V1= X ml** (what we are looking for), C2= 0.5 M, V2 = 100 mL

2 x X = 0.5 x 100

**X = 25 mL**
